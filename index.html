what do we see in a mirror

Mirrors have fascinated people for millennia.

When people are made to be self-aware, they are likelier to stop and think about what they are doing

the Socratic notion that you cannot know or appreciate others until you know yourself

self awareness moves people toward acting in more desirable ways of behaving.

when we see our reflection, what do we to see? Are we able to see what we are shown?
